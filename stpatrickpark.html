Discover the Hidden Gems of St. Patrick's Park

To explore the park's features in detail, a comprehensive survey was conducted using the Mergin Map app. This digital tool allowed us to accurately map and document various aspects of the park, including:

  • Plant Species: From towering trees to delicate wildflowers, we identified and mapped the park's rich biodiversity.
  • Park Infrastructure: Benches, pathways, and other amenities were meticulously documented.
  • Litter and Other Issues: We highlighted areas requiring attention, such as litter and damaged infrastructure.

    • Unveiling the Map

    The resulting map offers a visual representation of St. Patrick's Park. By clicking on different features, you can learn more about their specific characteristics.

Challenges and Future Directions

While this project provides a valuable overview of the park, there are always opportunities for improvement. Challenges included accurately categorizing natural features and technical aspects of map creation.

  • Incorporate scientific names for plant species.
  • Add quantitative data, such as height and spread measurements.
  • Expand the survey to include other urban green spaces.
By refining our methods and expanding the scope of our work, we hope to create even more detailed and informative maps of our urban environments.
