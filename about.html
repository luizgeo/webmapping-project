About Our Project

Welcome to our Web Map Project! Our goal is to provide insightful and interactive maps that highlight various environmental and geographic topics. Each map is meticulously crafted to offer unique insights, making complex data accessible and engaging for everyone.

Meet the Team

We are a team of passionate geographers, GIS experts, and environmental scientists. Our combined expertise allows us to bring diverse perspectives and detailed analysis to each map, empowering our users to understand and engage with the topics more deeply.

Our Approach

Using tools like QGIS, we strive to make geographic information more accessible. Our maps focus on critical areas such as ecosystem services, flood risks, and urban planning, aiming to support education and decision-making.
