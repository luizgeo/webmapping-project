About This Project

This website showcases a selection of interactive maps created as part of the Web Mapping module of our Digital Mapping and GIS course. By utilizing powerful, open-source tools like QGIS and qgis2web, we aim to enhance our ability to analyze and share spatial data with the world.

Each map offers a unique perspective on various geographical and environmental themes, from park's infrastructure to risk assessment and ecosystem services. Explore these interactive maps to gain a deeper understanding of our planet and the challenges it faces.
